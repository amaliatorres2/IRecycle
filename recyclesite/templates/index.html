New York City has no landfills or incinerators, yet residents produce 12,000 tons of waste everyday


Our Mission

Our mission is to help NYC residents become eco-friendlly.

You Should Use iRecycle if you:

1. Are a New York City resident unsure of where your recyclable trash belongs

2. Want to be part of a community of Recyclers

3. Care about the environment

What Are We Sorting?

  • Plastic

    2.5 million plastic bottles are thrown away every hour in America.

  • Glass

    A modern glass bottle would take 4,000 years or more to decompose

  • Cans

    Aluminum can be recycled forever without any loss of quality.

  • Cardboard

    One ton of recycled cardboard saves 9 cubic yards of landfill space.

  • Compost

    Nearly half of the food in the U.S. goes to waste

  • Non-Recyclables

    The average person generates 4.4 pounds of solid waste every day
